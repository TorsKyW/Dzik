Rules


Numbers will appear on screen in a chosen tempo. If the number on screen has a 7 in it or is divisible by 7, pressing the Boar will grant a point. Skipping a number that does not meet the requirements will also grant a point. Pressing a wrong number will deduct a point. Should you click a wrong number 3 times, the game will end. Missing a number with a 7 or that is divisible by 7 will instantly finish the game.
